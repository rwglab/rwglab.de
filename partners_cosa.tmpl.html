CoSA Research Group
University of Applied Sciences, Lübeck

The Research Group CoSA (Communications - Systems - Applications), a Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the Fachhochschule Lübeck, deals with Communication Networks, Distributed Systems and their Applications.

Main focus of research are modern structures like wireless mobile networks and sensor networks. Of special interest are sensor networks in various application fields reaching from medical applications to underwater technologies.
